RPE Reference Guide

Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE) Scale

Use this scale to rate the intensity of your training sessions from 1 (very easy) to 10 (maximum effort).

1-2
Very Easy
Barely any effort, could continue for hours
3-4
Easy
Light effort, comfortable pace
5-6
Moderate
Noticeable effort, moderate challenge
7-8
Hard
Challenging effort, getting difficult
9-10
Very Hard / Maximum
Near maximum to absolute maximum effort

Optimal Training Ranges

ACWR (Acute:Chronic Workload Ratio)
0.8 - 1.3 Optimal 0.7 - 0.8, 1.3 - 1.5 Caution <0.7, >1.5 High Risk
